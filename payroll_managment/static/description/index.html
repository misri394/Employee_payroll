Payroll Management Odoo App

Welcome to the Payroll Management Odoo App showcase! This app is designed to streamline your payroll process and make managing employee salaries and taxes easier.

Key Features:

  • Employee management
  • Salary calculation
  • Tax management
  • Payroll reports

Installation Instructions:

  1. Download the Payroll Management Odoo App from the Odoo Apps Store.
  2. Install the app on your Odoo instance.
  3. Configure the app settings according to your payroll requirements.
  4. You're ready to manage your payroll efficiently with Odoo!
